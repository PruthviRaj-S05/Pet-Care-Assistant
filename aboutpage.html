About Us

At Pet Care Assistant, we are passionate about pets and their well-being. Founded by Pruthvi, our service was started with the mission of providing reliable, loving, and professional care to your furry family members.

Whether it’s daily care, grooming, or helping manage pet schedules, we aim to make your and your pet’s life easier. We believe every pet deserves to be happy, healthy, and cared for like family.

Our Goals

  • Provide stress-free care services tailored to each pet's needs.
  • Build trust with pet owners through reliability and compassion.
  • Design an intuitive digital assistant to help manage pet care tasks.
  • Grow a loving community of pet owners and caregivers.
