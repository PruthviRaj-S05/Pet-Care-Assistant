Bathing

  • Use lukewarm water and pet-safe shampoo
  • Protect ears to avoid infections
  • Dry thoroughly with towel or pet dryer

Hair Grooming

  • Brush regularly to prevent mats and tangles
  • Trim excess hair around eyes and paws
  • Use proper tools for coat type (slicker, comb, etc.)

Dental Hygiene

  • Brush teeth with pet-safe toothpaste weekly
  • Dental treats help reduce tartar buildup
  • Check gums for redness or swelling

Skin & Coat Health

  • Check for parasites like fleas or ticks
  • Moisturize dry or flaky skin with vet-approved products
  • Shiny coat indicates good nutrition and care

Eye Cleaning

  • Wipe tear stains with soft, damp cloth
  • Use sterile wipes if needed
  • Look out for redness or unusual discharge

Nail Care

  • Trim nails regularly to prevent overgrowth
  • Avoid cutting into the quick (sensitive area)
  • Smooth sharp edges with a file if needed
