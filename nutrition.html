Balanced Diet

  • Includes protein, fats, carbs, vitamins & minerals
  • Tailored to species, age, and activity level
  • Supports growth, energy, and immunity

Quality Ingredients

  • Whole meats (chicken, beef, salmon)
  • Natural sources of vitamins and fiber
  • Free from artificial colors and fillers

Types of Food

  • Dry kibble for convenience and dental benefits
  • Wet food for hydration and taste
  • Raw or homemade under vet supervision

Food to Avoid

  • Chocolate, onions, garlic, grapes/raisins
  • Xylitol (in sugar-free products)
  • Fatty scraps or cooked bones

Hydration

  • Clean, fresh water always available
  • Increased intake with dry diets or hot weather
  • Monitor for signs of dehydration

Treats & Supplements

  • Treats should be less than 10% of daily calories
  • Use treats for training and bonding
  • Supplements (like omega-3s) only if needed
